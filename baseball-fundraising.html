Baseball Fundraising FAQ

What are the best baseball fundraising ideas?

Custom baseball merchandise is the most effective fundraising method. Teams typically raise $2,000-$5,000+ selling custom jerseys, hats, hoodies, and gear with their team logo. It's much more profitable than car washes or candy sales.

How much can a baseball team raise for tournaments?

Most baseball teams raise $3,000-$7,000 per season through custom merchandise sales. This easily covers tournament fees, travel expenses, and new equipment without requiring volunteers or upfront costs.

What baseball merchandise sells best for fundraising?

Custom baseball jerseys, team hats, hoodies, and performance shirts are the top sellers. Parents and supporters love high-quality gear they can wear to games and around town.

When is the best time for baseball fundraising?

Pre-season (January-March) and mid-season work best. This gives you time to raise money for tournament fees and equipment before the busy game schedule starts.

How long does baseball merchandise take to ship?

All custom baseball gear is printed and shipped within 3-5 business days. Perfect for getting merchandise before big tournaments or end-of-season celebrations.

Can we customize designs for our baseball team?

Absolutely! We create custom designs featuring your team name, logo, colors, and any special elements you want. Every design is unique to your baseball team.
