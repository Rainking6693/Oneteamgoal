The Real Problems with Car Wash Fundraising

Volunteer Coordination Nightmare

Trying to get 20+ parents to show up on the same day is like herding cats. Someone always cancels last minute, leaving you scrambling.

Weather Dependency Stress

Rain = cancelled event = no money raised. You've planned for weeks and suddenly you're rescheduling everything because of clouds.

Tiny Profit Margins

After buying supplies, paying for location, and accounting for the time invested, you're lucky to clear $300. That barely covers new equipment.

One-Day-Only Revenue

All that planning and stress for just one day of sales. If turnout is low, you're stuck with whatever you made - no second chances.

Limited Customer Base

Only people who need their car washed will stop by. That's a tiny fraction of potential supporters who would buy team merchandise.

Coach Burnout

You became a coach to teach kids sports, not to become an event coordinator managing volunteers and supplies every few months.

How Custom Merchandise Solves Every Problem

Zero Volunteer Coordination

Everything is automated. No begging parents to help, no last-minute cancellations, no managing volunteers. Just share your link and watch sales roll in.

Weather-Proof Fundraising

Your online store works 24/7, rain or shine. Supporters can buy merchandise anytime, anywhere, from their phone or computer.

10x Higher Profit Margins

Teams typically raise $2,000-$5,000+ because parents actually want high-quality team gear. Much better ROI than car washes.

Ongoing Revenue Stream

Your store stays open for weeks or months. Supporters can share with friends and family, creating ongoing sales beyond your immediate team circle.

Massive Customer Base

Every parent, grandparent, friend, and supporter wants team merchandise. Way bigger market than people needing car washes.

Coach Peace of Mind

Set it up once and focus on coaching. No event management, no supply runs, no volunteer coordination. Just coaching your team.
